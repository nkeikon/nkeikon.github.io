Yesterday I had an interesting discussion with Google and other experts on AI agents' discoverability of knowledge and skills via skills . md. We discussed how certain skill implementations, documentation, and demonstrations can become more reachable and deterministic, and whether they may quickly become obsolete and eventually be replaced by AI agents themselves. I can see that understanding how AI agents prioritize certain information over others is critical for the private sector and their survival. I also wonder how scientific knowledge and information from academia and government agencies will position themselves and carry weight in this new world. The world where users no longer have to read a 500-page document, but we rely on AI agents to read them correctly, reconcile conflicting information across sources, and sustain the integrity of the knowledge within.
