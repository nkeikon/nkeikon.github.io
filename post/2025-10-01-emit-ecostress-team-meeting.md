This week I attended the EMIT/ECOSTREESS annual team meeting in Pasadena! It was very inspiring to hear interesting and creative research utilising those sensors.

I couldn't resist but look at my area's Dutch Slough Tidal Marsh Restoration Project via the eye of ECOSTRESS... Rather than a per pixel before and after, I'm contrasting each site with its nearby surroundings at each time. Restored vs not yet restored areas show clear Land Surface Temperature contrasts. Evapotranspiration is lower than neighbours before restoration and higher after. Restoration shifts the site from water limited to energy limited, and plants transpire more.

For more information about ECOSTRESS: https://lnkd.in/gjbN9DNA
