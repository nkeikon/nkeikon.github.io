LANDSAT 9 captured the burning vegetation on Santa Rosa Island, Channel Islands, on May 16th. The fire started the early morning of May 15th. By today, around 4,060 ha had been burned (CALFIRE). The Channel Islands host 621 native plant species, 137 of which are endemic, including six found only on Santa Rosa.
